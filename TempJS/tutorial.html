In order to control the character you need to use the arrow keys to move, and the control key to interact. You can interact with many objects in the room.

The library allows you to create new notes, these are saved on our server.

You can also use the canvas in order to paint your note within the bounderies.

This app is currently in its beta phase, things may change in the future.

We don't recommend you use BitPad for important documents.
